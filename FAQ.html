FQAs

What courses are offered at ICMS College?

We offer BCA, B.Com, BBA, DCA, Tally, Advanced Excel, Programming and many more courses.

What is the admission process?

Fill the admission form, submit documents, pay fees and confirm your admission.

What is the eligibility criteria?

Eligibility depends on course. Most require 10th or 12th pass.

Do you provide placement support?

Yes, we provide placement assistance and career guidance to students.

How can I contact the college?

You can contact us via phone, email or visit our contact page.

What is the fee structure of courses?

The fee structure varies depending on the course. Please contact the college or visit the admissions page for detailed information.

Are online classes available?

Yes, we offer both online and offline classes for selected courses..

Do you provide certificates after course completion?

Yes, students receive a certificate after successfully completing their course.

What are the class timings?

We offer flexible timings including morning, afternoon, and evening batches.

Is there any scholarship or discount available?

Yes, we provide scholarships and discounts for meritorious and economically weaker students.
